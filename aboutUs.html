About Us

Helping Children Grow in Christ Through Visual Stories & Faith-Based Learning

Who We Are

Yeshu Ke Nanhe Dost is a faith-based platform created to teach children about Jesus Christ through visual stories, blogs, Bible lessons, and creative content. Our mission is to make the Word of God simple, engaging, and joyful for young hearts.

We believe that when children learn about Jesus at an early age, they develop strong spiritual roots that guide them throughout their lives. Using modern digital tools, we present Bible stories in a visual and easy-to-understand way so children can grow in faith, love, and obedience.

Our vision is to create a safe and positive online space where children and families can learn, explore, and experience God’s love together.

Founder’s Message

I am thankful to God for giving me the vision to start this platform for children. In today’s world, technology plays a major role in shaping young minds, and we want to use it to bring children closer to Jesus Christ.

This website is created to help children understand the Bible through visual storytelling, meaningful blogs, and creative lessons. Our goal is to present God’s Word in a simple, loving, and joyful way.

I pray that this platform becomes a blessing for every child and family and helps raise a generation that loves Jesus and walks in His truth.
