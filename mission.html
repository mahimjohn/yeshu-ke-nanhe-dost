Who We Are

A Little Place Where
Faith Meets Joy

Yeshu Ke Nanhe Dost — meaning "Jesus's Little Friends" — is a children's Christian ministry lovingly created for young hearts. Through Bible stories, songs, puzzles, quizzes, colouring sheets, and video clips, children can discover the love of the Lord Jesus in a fun and simple way.

Our goal is simple: that every child not only understands the Bible but also carries its teachings into their daily lives — love, kindness, honesty, forgiveness, and gratitude. This is a place where faith and delight come together.

This ministry is a home for young children, parents, Sunday school teachers, and Christian families everywhere.
