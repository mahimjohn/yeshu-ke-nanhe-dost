Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 20, 2026

At Yeshu Ke Nanhe Dost, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how we keep it safe. By using our website and services, you agree to this policy.

1 Who We Are

Yeshu Ke Nanhe Dost ("we", "us", "our") is a children's Christian ministry based in India that provides Bible stories, educational content, and faith-based products for children. Our website is located at yeshukenanhedost.com.

For privacy-related questions, contact us at: contact@yeshukenanhedost.com

2 Information We Collect

Information you provide to us

  • Phone number — collected when you create an account or log in via OTP
  • Name — provided by you when registering
  • Delivery addresses — entered when placing an order (house/flat number, street, city, pincode)
  • Profile photo — optionally uploaded by you in Settings
  • Payment information — processed securely by Razorpay; we do not store your card or UPI details

Information collected automatically

  • Order history — items purchased, amounts, delivery status
  • Cart data — stored locally on your device (localStorage)
  • Notification preferences — stored locally on your device
  • Language preference — stored locally on your device

3 How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Process and deliver your orders
  • Send order confirmation and delivery updates
  • Respond to your queries and provide customer support
  • Improve our website, products, and services
  • Send information about new stories, products, or offers — only if you have opted in

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party for marketing purposes.

4 How We Store Your Information

Your account information (name, phone number, addresses, profile photo) is stored securely on Google Firebase (Firebase Realtime Database and Firebase Storage), which is hosted on Google Cloud infrastructure with industry-standard encryption.

Your cart, language preferences, and notification settings are stored in your browser's local storage on your own device and are never transmitted to our servers.

Payment transactions are processed by Razorpay and are subject to Razorpay's own security standards and privacy practices.

6 Children's Privacy

Our website is a children's ministry platform. While our content is designed for children, account registration requires a phone number and is intended to be used by parents or guardians on behalf of their children.

We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children under the age of 13 without parental consent. If you believe your child has provided personal information without your knowledge, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

7 Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction — update your name or address through your account Settings
  • Deletion — delete your account from the Settings page, which permanently removes your data from our systems
  • Opt out — turn off notification preferences in Settings at any time

To exercise any of these rights, you can use the Settings page on our website or contact us at contact@yeshukenanhedost.com.

8 Cookies & Local Storage

We do not use tracking cookies. We use your browser's localStorage (a similar technology) to remember your login session, cart items, and preferences. This data stays on your device and is never sent to advertising networks.

9 Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information. Your phone number is used for OTP-based authentication through Firebase, which uses industry-standard security. Payments are handled entirely by Razorpay's PCI-DSS compliant systems.

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We encourage you to keep your device secure and not share your OTP with anyone.

10 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically. Continued use of our website after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
